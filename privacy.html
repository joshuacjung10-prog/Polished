Polished

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

Polished is an AI-powered confidence coaching Chrome extension. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use Polished.

What data we access

When you start a session, Polished accesses the following:

  • Your camera — to capture still images for posture and expression analysis
  • Your microphone — to transcribe your speech for voice analysis

How we use your data

Images captured from your camera and transcriptions of your speech are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI for real time analysis. This analysis is used solely to generate confidence coaching feedback for you.

We do not store, record, or retain any video, audio, or images from your sessions. All data is processed in real time and discarded immediately after feedback is generated.

What we do not do

  • We do not store your camera footage or audio recordings
  • We do not sell your data to any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your data with anyone except Anthropic for the purpose of AI analysis
  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information

Third party services

Polished uses the following third party services:

  • Anthropic Claude AI — processes images and speech transcripts to generate coaching feedback. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at anthropic.com/privacy
  • Chrome Speech Recognition API — used to transcribe your speech locally in your browser before sending to Claude for analysis

Data security

All data sent from Polished to our servers is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not maintain any database of user information.

Children's privacy

Polished is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
